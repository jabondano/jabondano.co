things I believe

  • Small teams should be dangerous. A 15 person team can move like a 150 person one. Not by working harder, but by eliminating the work that shouldn't exist.
  • AI doesn't replace people. It replaces the soul crushing parts. The copy paste, the status updates, the hunting for information. What's left is judgment, creativity, relationships. The stuff worth doing.
  • Systems beat willpower. Build the environment. The behavior follows.
  • Optimism is a strategy. Not blind hope‚Äîthe belief that your actions matter and most problems yield to effort.
  • Document everything. What's in your head is a liability. What's written down becomes training data. A voice note, a napkin sketch, a diagram‚Äîit all compounds.
  • Hold beliefs loosely. Strong opinions, weakly held. The goal is to be less wrong over time, not to be right.
