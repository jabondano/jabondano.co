bio

Joaquin Abondano is the Chief Operating Officer at Innovative Eyewear Inc. (NASDAQ: LUCY), where he leads operations for the world's first ChatGPT-enabled smart eyewear company. Since joining in 2023, he has driven 163% year-over-year revenue growth and expanded distribution to 27+ countries.

Previously, he co-founded Groovy Toys and scaled it to $20M+ in revenue, launching over 500 consumer products including mobile apps, multiplayer games, and IoT devices. He holds a BS in Management with a Finance minor from Purdue University.
